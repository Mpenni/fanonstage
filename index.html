FAN on STAGE

"In the future, everyone will be world-famous for 15 minutes" (A.Warhol)

Inspired by Warhol's famous phrase, we have collected the moments when fans like you were invited on stage by your favorite singers for their moment of stardom.

Whether it's dancing on stage with a legendary rock band, singing a song with a famous pop singer, or just hugging your idol in the spotlight, we've captured all those magical moments for you.
